What we do?

BELIEVER Compliance & Sustainability is the world leading organization engaged with audit, inspection, testing, certification, training, and research & development. Established in 2020 with a group of experienced compliance & sustainability professionals, it aims to transform factories, manufacturing units, trading houses as well as corporate offices from non-compliant to compliant status by all of its services. And by achieving these services, they can meet the requirements of local labor law and various buyers’ requirements and internationally acclaimed safety standards. BELIEVER helps improve the value of the organizational corporate social responsibilities towards man, machines, technology as well as environment to make a safer, better and a sustainable world.

BELIEVER offers its services for both the public and private sectors and for all sorts of industries such as: Manufacturing, Construction & Engineering, IT, Service-oriented industries, Ed-Tech, Healthcare, Agro, Fin-Tech, Logistic, Trading etc.

Vision Statement

We envision a world where we have empowered it with maintained compliance and worked hard and smartly to attain sustainability enabling the future generation of the manufacturing and corporate world to become more effective and efficient contributors to society and the world.

Mission Statement

Aiding the manufacturing units and corporate businesses reach sustainability through compliance and eco-friendly practices so that our effort leaves a long lasting positive effect in the industry.
